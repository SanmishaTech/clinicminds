Admin View (Full Financial Control)
Franchise Management: Monitors fees and payments from all clinic locations.
Master Pricing: Sets the Rates and GST for all services and medicines.
Global Sales: Oversees every invoice and transport fee in the network.
Aggregated Reports: Access to global Sales and Day Book summaries.



Member View (Franchise/Staff - Unit Specific)
Patient Billing: Generates bills and receipts for their specific clinic.
Day Book: Manages the daily cash and digital collections.
Purchases: Tracks the cost of stock being bought from the central Admin.
Patient Balances: Monitors "Credit" or outstanding amounts for their local patients.
Local Reports: Views only their specific clinic's performance reports.
Doctor View
primarily medical-focused, but can see Service Rates and Medicine MRPs when creating consultations or prescribing.
